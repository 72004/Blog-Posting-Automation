1. How to Creatively Design a Living Room with an Off-Center TV in

Designing a living room around an off-center TV requires thoughtful spatial planning and creative furnishings. In 2026, consider incorporating asymmetrical seating arrangements that direct focus towards the screen without dominating the room.
